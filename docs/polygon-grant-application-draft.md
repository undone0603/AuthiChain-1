## Project Name
AuthiChain — Blockchain Product Authentication Protocol

## One-Liner
AuthiChain verifies product authenticity in <2 seconds using Polygon-anchored provenance certificates and AI-generated QR art (QRON).

## Problem
- $2.3T in counterfeit goods traded annually (OECD 2025)
- Cannabis dispensaries lose 15-20% revenue to diversion/counterfeiting
- EU Digital Product Passport mandate begins July 2026 (batteries Feb 2027)
- Existing solutions (Verisart, Chronicled) cost $0.50-2.00/verification — prohibitive at scale

## Solution
AuthiChain provides blockchain-verified product certificates on Polygon at $0.004/seal:
- **Scan → Verify → Trust** in 2.1 seconds, any phone, no app
- **QRON**: AI-generated art QR codes that brands actually want to display (78% higher scan rates)
- **Five-Agent Consensus**: 5 independent AI agents must agree before marking authentic
- **METRC-compatible**: Works alongside existing cannabis compliance systems
