## AuthiChain Unique Advantages

1. **Multi-industry** — cannabis + luxury + pharma + electronics + fashion + food (10 verticals)
2. **AI art QR (QRON)** — competitors use plain QR codes; QRON makes QR codes scannable art
3. **Self-serve PLG** — qron.space free tier → paid, no sales call required
4. **EU DPP ready** — compliance with EU 2024/1781 (batteries Feb 2027, textiles mid-2027)
5. **$0.004/seal** — 10-100x cheaper than enterprise competitors
6. **2.1 second verify** — fastest in market
